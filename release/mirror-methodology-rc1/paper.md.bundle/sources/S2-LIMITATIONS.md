# Limitations

MIRROR supports local evidence replay; it does not establish truth, completeness, novelty, authorship, legal compliance, peer review, public timestamping, or publication readiness.

This release candidate is not a standards proposal, assurance service, audit report, legal opinion, trust-service output, or compliance certificate. It is a research-method package for review.

Known limitations before public use:

- citations and related work need expansion;
- the minimal example is illustrative, not a validation study;
- source-retention policy needs human review for licensing and redistribution;
- bundle verification checks bytes and schema relationships, not substantive claim quality;
- external timestamping/signing is intentionally absent.
